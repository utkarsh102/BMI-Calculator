Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure that uses a person's height and weight to estimate body fat and assess whether someone is underweight, healthy weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI (Kg/m²) Interpretation
< 18.5 Underweight
18.5-24.9 Normal
25-29.9 Over Weight
≥ 30 Obese
30-34.9 Class 1 Obese
35-39.9 Class 2 Obese
≥ 40 Class 3 Obese

BMI Categories and Health Risks:

Underweight (BMI < 18.5): May experience nutritional deficiencies and other health problems.

Healthy Weight (BMI 18.5-24.9): Considered a healthy weight range with minimal health risks.

Overweight (BMI 25-29.9): May have an increased risk of developing health problems like heart disease and type 2 diabetes.

Obese (BMI 30 or higher): Significantly increased risk of developing serious health conditions.
